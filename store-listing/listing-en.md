## Full description (≤ 4000 chars)
"Al-Du'a wa al-Ziyara" brings together selected supplications and ziyarat from the book by the religious authority Sayyid Muhammad al-Husayni al-Shirazi, in an elegant Arabic interface that works completely offline.

Features:
• Library organized by the book's chapters: post-prayer dhikr, morning/evening du'as, daily du'as for each weekday, Friday observances, famous du'as (Kumayl, Tawassul, Nudba, Iftitah, Ahd, Sahar, Faraj), munajat, du'as for needs, Ramadan observances, and ziyarat (Ashura, Warith, Amin Allah, Arba'een, al-Jami'a al-Kabira, Aal Yasin, Hadith al-Kisa, and ziyarat of the Prophet and Lady Fatima).
• Smart full-text search that ignores diacritics and normalizes hamza forms.
• Comfortable reader: font size control, show/hide diacritics, copy, and favorites.
• Prayer times computed astronomically for your location (Ja'fari method and others) with the shar'i midnight.
• On-device prayer-time notifications.
• Hijri calendar with Twelver Shia occasions (births, martyrdoms, eids) with a day-adjustment option for local moon sighting.
• Qur'an istikhara (khira) with its etiquette.
• Tasbih of Lady Fatima (as) with a circular counter.
• Light and dark themes; all data stored locally on your device only.

Note: Texts follow widely-circulated versions and may differ slightly from the printed edition; prayer times are approximate.

The app requires no internet and collects no personal data.
